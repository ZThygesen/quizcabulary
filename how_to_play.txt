How to play:

Select 1 Player, 2 Players, or Time Trial

1 Player: Work against the clock to answer as many questions correctly as possible in 60 seconds.

2 Players: Compete against a friend and see who can get more questions correct in 60 seconds.

Time Trial: Race to see how fast you can get 10 questions correct!


Select Mode 1 or Mode 2

Mode 1: You are given a word and multiple definitions. Choose the definition that matches the word.

Mode 2: You are given a definition and multiple words. Choose the word that matches the definition.


Select Difficulty

Easy: You are given 3 answer choices.

Medium: You are given 4 answer choices.

Hard: You are given 5 answer choices. 


Exit the game at any point by clicking on the X in the upper right hand corner.
